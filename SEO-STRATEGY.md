## 1. Executive Summary & Vision

ScentBazaar is Dubai's only dedicated B2B fragrance ingredient supplier with a physical showroom in the historic Naif Souq, Deira. With 2,000+ fragrances, 1 kg MOQ, IFRA/GC-MS documentation, and 172 verified Google reviews, ScentBazaar has every ingredient for SEO dominance — it is simply not yet visible online at the scale the business deserves.

**The opportunity:** No competitor directly targets B2B fragrance ingredient supply keywords in the UAE market. The space is dominated by luxury retail brands (Ajmal, Arabian Oud) that have no B2B ingredient content, and international suppliers (Perfumers World, Brambleberry, New Directions Aromatics) that have no UAE presence. ScentBazaar can own this niche with focused content and technical execution.

**12-Month Vision:** Rank #1–3 for all primary B2B fragrance ingredient keywords in the UAE, establish thought leadership in the regional perfumery and candle-making communities, and generate a consistent inbound pipeline of wholesale inquiries from the website.

**Strategic Priorities:**
1. Fix critical E-E-A-T gaps (About Us, Reviews page, credentials)
2. Launch blog to capture informational keywords and build topical authority
3. Dominate local SEO for Deira/Dubai fragrance supply searches
4. Expand Arabic language presence for the regional wholesale market
5. Optimize for AI search (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews)

## 2. Target Audience & Buyer Personas

### Persona 1 — The Startup Perfumer
- **Who:** Individual or small team launching a perfume brand in the UAE or GCC
- **Goals:** Find quality fragrance oils at low MOQ; understand formulation and compliance
- **Pain points:** High MOQs elsewhere; uncertainty about IFRA compliance; no local technical support
- **Search behavior:** "fragrance oil supplier UAE", "where to buy perfume oil Dubai", "1kg fragrance oil minimum order"
- **Content needed:** Product pages, IFRA guides, formulation blog posts, starter kit content
- **Decision trigger:** Low MOQ (1 kg), IFRA documentation, ability to visit showroom

### Persona 2 — The Candle Business Owner
- **Who:** UAE-based candle maker (home business to medium enterprise)
- **Goals:** Source consistent, high-throw fragrance oils; understand load rates; find candle-safe options
- **Pain points:** Inconsistent suppliers; no local candle fragrance specialists; sourcing packaging separately
- **Search behavior:** "candle fragrance oil UAE", "fragrance oil for candles Dubai", "candle fragrance supplier"
- **Content needed:** Candle fragrance category page, load rate guides, fragrance families for candles, soy wax compatibility
- **Decision trigger:** Candle-specific expertise, product consistency, one-stop-shop for fragrance + wicks + jars

### Persona 3 — The Private Label Brand
- **Who:** Entrepreneur or SME creating a private label perfume, home fragrance, or cosmetics line
- **Search behavior:** "private label perfume Dubai", "custom perfume manufacturer UAE", "white label fragrance UAE"
- **Goals:** End-to-end supply chain; branding and packaging support; regulatory compliance for export
- **Pain points:** Finding a supplier who handles both ingredients AND packaging; MOQ flexibility
- **Content needed:** Private label service page, case studies, brand launch guide, packaging catalog
- **Decision trigger:** End-to-end capability, showroom visit, references/reviews

### Persona 4 — The Cosmetics/Soap Manufacturer
- **Who:** Cosmetics, soap, or personal care product manufacturer in UAE or region
- **Search behavior:** "cosmetic fragrance supplier Dubai", "soap fragrance oil UAE", "diffuser base supplier UAE"
- **Goals:** Reliable bulk supply of cosmetic-grade ingredients; SDS and IFRA documentation
- **Pain points:** Finding compliant ingredients; documentation for export markets
- **Content needed:** Cosmetic base pages, SDS downloads, bulk pricing info, compliance guides
- **Decision trigger:** Documentation quality, bulk pricing, reliability of supply

## 5. E-E-A-T Building Plan

ScentBazaar's biggest SEO weakness is E-E-A-T signals. The site has strong real-world credibility (172 reviews, physical showroom, IFRA documentation) but none of it is surfaced on the website.

### Experience Signals
- **About Us page** (Priority 1): Company founding story, years in Deira, showroom photos, team photos
- **Showroom content**: Add showroom images to homepage and relevant pages with descriptive alt text
- **Process transparency**: Show how fragrances are sourced, tested, documented

### Expertise Signals
- **Blog authorship**: All blog posts attributed to named authors with brief bios
- **Technical content**: IFRA compliance guides, GC-MS report explainers, formulation ratios — demonstrate deep knowledge
- **Fragrance catalog**: 2,000+ catalog signals expertise in sourcing and curation

### Authoritativeness Signals
- **Reviews page**: Dedicate a page to the 172 Google reviews with curated testimonials
- **Media mentions**: Seek coverage in UAE business press, perfumery publications
- **Partnerships**: Display any certifications, partnerships (IFRA membership, Dubai Chamber, etc.)
- **Case studies**: 2 client success stories (private label brands launched with ScentBazaar)

### Trustworthiness Signals
- **IFRA/GC-MS documentation**: Prominently surface availability of safety documentation
- **Physical address**: Ensure NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistent across all pages and directories
- **Privacy policy & Terms**: Ensure present and linked from footer
- **HTTPS**: Already in place — maintain

### E-E-A-T Priority Actions
1. Create About Us page with full company story, team, showroom photos
2. Create Reviews/Testimonials page with curated Google review excerpts
3. Add author bios to all blog posts
4. Add IFRA compliance badge/mention to relevant product pages
5. Surface the 172 reviews count on homepage with link to Google profile

## 10. AI Search / GEO Optimization

AI search tools (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews) are increasingly the first touchpoint for B2B research queries. ScentBazaar needs to be cited in AI responses for fragrance supply questions.

### Current State
- `llms.txt` present (from previous audit)
- Basic schema implemented
- No structured Q&A content

### GEO Actions

**1. Structured Q&A Content**
Add explicit Q&A sections to key pages and blog posts targeting questions AI tools answer:
- "What is the minimum order quantity for fragrance oils in Dubai?"
- "Where can I buy IFRA-compliant fragrance oils in UAE?"
- "How do I start a private label perfume brand in Dubai?"
- "What is the difference between perfumers alcohol and carrier oil?"

**2. Passage-Level Citability**
Write content in discrete, quotable paragraphs. Each section should stand alone as a citable answer. Avoid burying key facts in long prose.

**3. FAQPage Schema**
Implement `FAQPage` schema on all service pages and blog posts — this feeds directly into AI training data and Google AI Overviews.

**4. llms.txt Maintenance**
Update `llms.txt` whenever new pages are added. Include all product categories, key facts (MOQ, location, certifications).

**5. Brand Mention Signals**
- Consistent brand name usage: always "Scent Bazaar" or "ScentBazaar"
- Encourage customers to mention ScentBazaar in their social posts and reviews
- Seek mentions in industry forums and community discussions

**6. AI Crawler Access**
Ensure `robots.txt` allows: GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, Googlebot-Extended
```
User-agent: GPTBot
Allow: /

User-agent: ClaudeBot
Allow: /

User-agent: PerplexityBot
Allow: /
```
