Can You Test Fragrances and Get Custom Blending In-Store?

While digital communication is excellent for repeat wholesale orders, we understand that fragrance is a deeply sensory experience. Visiting our Deira showroom allows you to access services that are difficult to replicate online.

  • Live Scent Profiling: Sit down with our evaluators to smell the difference between grades of Oud wood, or test the hot throw of our candle fragrance oils.
  • Hardware Fitment Tests: Bring your own bottle, or select one from our wholesale bottle supply to physically test the crimp atomizers and the weight of the Zamac caps before placing a bulk order.
  • On-Site Custom Blending: Work directly with our custom blending team to draft the initial architecture of your unique signature scent in real-time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to make an appointment to visit the store?

No, walk-ins are always welcome during our regular trading hours and no booking is required for a general browsing or sampling visit. Our team on the floor will assist you with fragrance selection, product information, and pricing on the spot. However, if you are looking to discuss a large-scale private label manufacturing project, commission a custom fragrance formula, or require a focused sit-down session with our lead formulator, we strongly recommend messaging us on WhatsApp in advance. This allows us to allocate a private consultation slot with the right team member, prepare relevant samples and pricing for your specific brief, and ensure you are not kept waiting if the floor is busy with other clients. For international buyers visiting Dubai with limited time, pre-booking a consultation is especially worthwhile.

Do you sell retail, or only wholesale?

Scent Bazaar is primarily a B2B wholesale operation, founded to serve fragrance brands, manufacturers, private label businesses, and retailers who buy in commercial quantities. Our standard minimum order quantity for oils is 100ml, which reflects our wholesale focus. That said, we do permit small-scale in-store purchasing — for example, a single 100g tola bottle of attar or a small quantity of a specific oil — for individuals who wish to personally evaluate our product quality, create personal blends, or test a fragrance in their own production process before committing to a wholesale order. We do not operate as a general retail perfume shop, and walk-in retail pricing differs from our B2B wholesale rates. For anyone considering a wholesale relationship, an in-store sampling visit is a sensible first step before placing a larger order.

Where is the best place to park near Souq Naif?

Deira can be highly congested, particularly during morning and early evening trading hours, and street parking around Souq Naif is limited and often occupied. The most reliable option is to use one of the RTA paid multi-storey parking structures in the Naif area — these are clearly signposted and offer hourly rates that are reasonable for a shopping or business visit. If you are travelling from elsewhere in Dubai, the most stress-free option is the Dubai Metro: take the Red Line to Baniyas Square station, from which our store is a short and straightforward walk through the souq. Taxis and ride-share apps such as Careem and Uber are also a practical choice — simply ask to be dropped at Souq Naif or give our address at Shop No. 47, Souq Naif, Deira. Avoid driving during Friday midday prayer hours when traffic and parking become particularly difficult.

What products can I purchase directly at the Deira store?

Our Deira location in Souq Naif stocks the complete Scent Bazaar product range available for immediate in-person purchase and same-day collection. This includes our wholesale fragrance oils — over 2,000 accords spanning Oriental, Arabic, Western, and fresh categories — as well as perfumers alcohol, reed diffuser bases, cosmetic lotion and cream bases, glass perfume bottles in a wide range of shapes and sizes, spray pumps, roller-ball applicators, caps and closures, bakhoor charcoal burners, and incense accessories. Packaging materials including outer cartons and labels are also available. Visiting the store allows you to smell oils directly on skin and on test strips, assess the quality of packaging components in hand, and make purchasing decisions with full sensory confidence rather than relying on descriptions alone. All in-stock items can be paid for and collected on the day of your visit.

Do you offer B2B consultations at the store for fragrance brands and manufacturers?

Yes. Our Deira headquarters offers structured B2B consultation sessions for fragrance brands, product manufacturers, contract packagers, and retailers looking to source at commercial scale. During a consultation, our team walks you through targeted fragrance sampling based on your brief, discusses custom blending or private label manufacturing options, advises on appropriate oil concentrations and carriers for your product application, and provides transparent bulk pricing across volume tiers. For brands building an entire fragrance range, we can work through a structured development brief covering scent positioning, fragrance family, and target market before selecting candidate oils for evaluation. Consultations are most productive when you arrive with a clear brief — even a rough one — covering your product type, target customer, and approximate order volumes. Book via WhatsApp to ensure our senior formulation staff are available during your visit.

What are the opening hours of the Scent Bazaar store in Deira?

Our Scent Bazaar store in Souq Naif, Deira is open Saturday through Thursday from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and on Friday from 4:00 PM to 8:00 PM, in line with the traditional trading pattern of the Naif souq area. Walk-in clients are welcome at any point during these hours for browsing, sampling, and purchasing. The store is typically busiest during mid-morning and late afternoon on weekdays, so if you prefer a quieter visit with more staff attention, arriving shortly after opening on a weekday morning is a good strategy. For private label consultations, custom formulation discussions, or large wholesale negotiations, we strongly recommend confirming your visit time via WhatsApp in advance so the appropriate team member is on-site and available. Hours may vary during UAE public holidays, so please check with us if visiting around Eid, National Day, or New Year.

How many fragrance oils can I sample during a single store visit?

There is no formal limit to the number of oils you can smell during a visit, but olfactory fatigue sets in after extended sampling, so quality of evaluation matters more than quantity. In practice, we guide clients through up to 20 to 30 targeted oils per session, selected based on your brief, which gives you enough variety to make confident shortlisting decisions without overwhelming your nose. We use coffee beans as a palate cleanser between fragrance families to extend your effective sampling window. For clients covering a large category — for example, sourcing both Oriental and fresh Western accords for a product range — we recommend splitting the visit into two sessions on different days if time permits. Arriving with a prepared brief covering your product application, target customer, fragrance family preferences, and budget per kilogram allows our team to pull the most relevant oils quickly rather than working through unrelated stock.

Can I take samples home from the Deira store?

Yes. We provide take-home fragrance oil samples for qualifying B2B clients, allowing you to conduct proper application testing in your own production environment before placing a wholesale order. Samples are dispensed into sealed glass vials, clearly labelled with the oil name and batch reference, so you can test them accurately in your candle wax at pour temperature, in a cosmetic lotion base, in alcohol dilution at your target EDP or EDT concentration, or in whatever manufacturing substrate is relevant to your product. This real-world testing phase is important — a fragrance that smells excellent on a test strip does not always perform identically in a specific wax or base, so we actively encourage clients to test before committing. Up to 12 oil varieties are provided free of charge per visit for genuine B2B buyers. Additional samples or specific oils not on the floor can be arranged via WhatsApp request ahead of your visit.

Is the Deira store accessible by Dubai Metro?

Yes. Our Souq Naif store is well served by Dubai's public transport network. The nearest Dubai Metro station is Baniyas Square on the Red Line, which is approximately a 5 to 10 minute walk from Souq Naif through the Deira streetscape. The Red Line connects directly to major hubs including Union Station, Deira City Centre, and Dubai Airport Terminal 1 and Terminal 3, making the store easily reachable from virtually anywhere in Dubai without a car. For those driving, paid RTA surface parking and multi-storey structures are available throughout the Naif and Deira area. Taxis and ride-share apps including Careem and Uber are also a practical option — direct the driver to Shop No. 47, Souq Naif, Deira. If you are travelling from Abu Dhabi or another emirate, inter-city bus services terminate at Union Square, from which the Metro is directly accessible.

Absolutely. Our team will measure, package, and label your order while you wait or browse, making store collection a fast and practical option for UAE-based buyers who need product quickly. For larger orders involving multiple drums, custom private label packaging, or items that require blending or preparation, a short lead time may apply — typically one to three business days. To avoid any waiting, we strongly recommend calling ahead on +971 54 772 7106 or messaging us on WhatsApp with your product list and quantities before you travel to the store. This ensures your order is ready for collection the moment you arrive, which is particularly valuable for buyers on a tight schedule or collecting large volumes that need to be loaded into a vehicle.
