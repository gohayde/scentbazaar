Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Who We Are

Scent Bazaar is a wholesale perfume oil and fragrance ingredient supplier based at Souq Naif, Shop 47, Deira, Dubai, UAE. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information you provide when visiting scentbazaar.co or contacting us.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information — name, email address, phone number, and business name when you submit an enquiry or contact us via WhatsApp.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent, referring URL, and browser type, collected automatically via Google Analytics.
  • Communications — messages you send us by email, WhatsApp, or through any contact form.

We do not collect payment card information directly. Any transactions are handled through secure third-party channels.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Improve our website based on usage analytics.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our pages. Google Analytics uses cookies — small text files stored on your device. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Data Retention

We retain enquiry and contact records for as long as necessary to fulfil your request and for a reasonable period thereafter for legitimate business purposes. Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics default retention settings.

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you by contacting us at the details below. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
