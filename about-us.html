About Scent Bazaar

Founded in 2025, Scent Bazaar is the trusted fragrance partner for emerging brands and DIY perfumers across 50+ countries. Our mission: deliver authentic, IFRA-compliant perfume oils with the technical expertise to help your fragrance business succeed.

Our Story

Scent Bazaar was founded in 2025 in the heart of Deira, Dubai's renowned fragrance district. Founded by Nawar, a qualified chemical engineer, the business was built on a clear mission: give emerging perfume brands and DIY fragrance makers access to professional-grade ingredients with no unnecessary barriers — flexible MOQs, transparent pricing, and real technical support.

From day one, Scent Bazaar set up in Souq Naif with a curated catalog of 2,000+ fragrance oils sourced from leading European and Arabic fragrance houses, paired with complete product formulation support and private label services.

Our commitment is simple: provide world-class fragrance ingredients and the expertise to use them — whether you're launching your first fragrance brand or scaling a multi-national perfume business.

Our Values

  • Authenticity: Every fragrance oil is tested for compliance with IFRA standards. We never compromise on quality.
  • Accessibility: Our MOQs start at 1kg, making professional-grade fragrance available to entrepreneurs of all sizes.
  • Expertise: Our team includes certified perfume chemists and fragrance specialists who guide customers through formulation challenges.
  • Reliability: We've built our reputation fast — 172+ verified 5-star reviews — by delivering on time, every time.
  • Sustainability: We partner with responsible fragrance producers and provide MSDS documentation for all international shipments.
