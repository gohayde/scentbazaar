## ⚡ Critical Success Factors

### 1. Author Attribution (E-E-A-T Signal)
- Blog posts: Name the author + credentials
- Videos: Say your name, title, role
- Reddit posts: Disclose "I work for Scent Bazaar"
- Why: AI systems weight named experts 3x more

### 2. Publication Dates
- Add: `<meta property="article:published_time" content="2026-03-17">` to all pages
- Why: Improves AI citation likelihood by 1.4x
- Effort: 2-3 hours for all pages

### 3. Community Authenticity
- Reddit: 90% helpful, 10% brand (no sales pitches)
- LinkedIn: Can be more promotional
- Respond within 24 hours to comments
- Disclose affiliation upfront

### 4. Consistency Over Perfection
- YouTube: Phone camera quality is fine
- Reddit: Genuine value > polish
- LinkedIn: Weekly is better than twice daily
- Blog: One post every 2-3 weeks beats sporadic

### 5. Patience (3-6 Month Timeline)
- AI platform lift takes time
- Wikipedia requires 2-3 months of press mentions
- Monthly monitoring keeps you on track
