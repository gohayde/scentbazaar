Industrial & Cosmetic Fragrance Oils

While fine perfumery requires highly specific French and Arabic concentrates, large-scale industrial manufacturing requires a different approach: absolute cost-efficiency, chemical stability, and sheer volume. ScentBazaar answers this demand with our specialized bulk fragrance oil UAE division, engineered specifically for non-perfume consumer packaged goods (FMCG).

When you source from ScentBazaar, you aren't dealing with a middle-man. You are tapping into a direct manufacturing supply chain capable of producing and delivering multi-ton pallets across the globe via our logistics hub in Deira, Dubai.

Global Logistics & Compliance

Massive Scale, Zero Compromise

The biggest challenge in industrial fragrance supply is consistency. An altered scent profile in a massive 5000L batch of detergent can be a costly disaster. We solve this through rigorous quality control processes executed right here in the UAE.

  • Standardized Drum Volumes: Our bulk oils are available in 25kg, 50kg, and 200kg industrial HDPE/Steel drums, fully sealed for maritime and air freight.
  • Global Regulatory Compliance: Every bulk shipment comes equipped with the necessary Material Safety Data Sheets (MSDS), Certificates of Analysis (COA), and strict adherence to IFRA guidelines.
  • Technical Chemical Support: We don't just sell the oil. If you require perfumers alcohol or powerful chemical fixatives to bind your bulk fragrances, our laboratory team can supply the exact solvents required for your unique application.

By leveraging Dubai’s world-class shipping infrastructure, ScentBazaar guarantees uninterrupted supply chains to Africa, Europe, the CIS regions, and the broader Middle East.

Frequently Asked Questions

What packaging sizes are available for bulk fragrance oils?

For industrial B2B clients, we package our bulk fragrance oils in heavy-duty HDPE jerry cans or steel drums ranging from 25 kilograms up to 200 kilograms per container.

Are your bulk oils suitable for candle making and soap?

Yes. Our industrial division formulates specifically for non-fine-fragrance applications. We offer specialized oils that perform excellently in soy wax, paraffin, cold-process soaps, and cosmetic lotions.

Do you offer bulk pricing or volume discounts?

Absolutely. Our pricing is highly tiered. Clients ordering by the drum (25kg+) or pallet will receive significant industrial discounts compared to our standard wholesale perfume oils in Dubai.

Can you handle ocean freight for massive orders?

Yes. Taking advantage of our strategic location in Dubai, ScentBazaar manages complex logistics including Less than Container Load (LCL) and Full Container Load (FCL) ocean freight to destination ports worldwide.
