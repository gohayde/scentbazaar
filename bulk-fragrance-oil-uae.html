Frequently Asked Questions

What packaging sizes are available for bulk fragrance oils?

For industrial B2B clients, we package our bulk fragrance oils in heavy-duty HDPE jerry cans or closed-head steel drums ranging from 25 kilograms up to 200 kilograms per container. HDPE jerry cans are the preferred format for air-freight-compatible quantities and for clients who handle the oil manually in production environments, as they are lighter and easier to pour from than steel drums. Steel drums are the standard for large ocean freight consignments where volume efficiency matters. For clients requiring smaller working quantities, we also supply in 5-litre and 10-litre HDPE containers, which are particularly useful for production trials or fragrance development work before scaling to drum volumes. All containers are labelled with full product identification, batch number, and MSDS reference in compliance with international dangerous goods transport requirements.

Are your bulk oils suitable for candle making and soap?

Yes. Our industrial division formulates specifically for non-fine-fragrance manufacturing applications, which have fundamentally different performance requirements to wearable perfumery. For candle production, we supply fragrance oils rated for use in soy wax, paraffin, and coconut wax blends, with verified flash points above the minimum safety threshold for your wax type and pour temperature. For soap manufacturing — both cold-process and hot-process — we offer oils that remain stable through the saponification reaction without accelerating trace or causing ricing. For cosmetic lotions and body care, we supply skin-safe compounds with confirmed dermal limits aligned to safety standards. We recommend telling us your specific application, wax type, or formulation base when enquiring, so we can direct you to the most technically appropriate oils from our catalogue of over 2,000 fragrances.

Do you offer bulk pricing or volume discounts?

Absolutely. Our pricing is structured across clearly defined volume tiers, and the per-kilogram cost drops substantially as order size increases. Clients ordering by the drum (25kg upwards) or full pallet receive significant industrial discounts compared to our standard wholesale perfume oils in Dubai pricing. The tiering accounts for both volume and fragrance type — synthetic-heavy accords and commercial-grade compounds are priced lower per kilogram than natural-rich or oud-based formulas, which use higher-cost raw materials. For clients placing repeat orders on a scheduled basis, we also offer contracted pricing arrangements that lock in rates against volume commitments, providing cost predictability for your production planning. Contact us via WhatsApp with your target fragrance profile, required volume, and intended application to receive a full tiered pricing breakdown without obligation.

Can you handle ocean freight for massive orders?

Yes. Our strategic location in Dubai — one of the world's busiest cargo transit hubs — gives us direct access to weekly vessel services serving ports across Africa, South Asia, Southeast Asia, Europe, and the Americas. Scent Bazaar manages complex logistics including Less than Container Load (LCL) consolidations for clients whose order volume does not justify a full container, and Full Container Load (FCL) shipments for pallet-scale or multi-tonne orders. We prepare all required export documentation including commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, and Dangerous Goods declarations where fragrance oils are classified as flammable liquids under IMDG regulations. For clients new to importing fragrance oils by ocean freight, our logistics team advises on the correct HS codes, DG packaging requirements, and destination port clearance procedures to avoid customs delays on arrival.

Can you develop a custom fragrance formula specifically for my manufacturing application?

Yes. Our in-house perfumers and formulation chemists work directly with industrial clients to develop bespoke fragrance compounds engineered for the demands of specific manufacturing conditions — whether that is high-temperature candle pouring, the alkaline environment of soap saponification, the mechanical shear of emulsion mixing, or the long-term stability required in rinse-off products. Unlike fine fragrance development, industrial fragrance formulation prioritises functional performance: the compound must survive your process intact and deliver the intended scent in the finished consumer product. We develop exclusive formulas that are locked to your brand and not supplied to other clients, ensuring your scent identity cannot be easily replicated by competitors sourcing from the same supplier. Development typically involves two to four sample iterations before formula sign-off, after which we produce and supply at your agreed bulk volumes.

Which FMCG sectors do your bulk industrial fragrance oils serve?

Our bulk fragrance oils serve a wide range of fast-moving consumer goods manufacturing sectors, each with distinct formulation requirements. In home care, we supply compounds for liquid detergents, powder detergents, fabric softeners, toilet blocks, and aerosol air fresheners — all requiring pH stability and substantivity on different surfaces. In personal care, we serve soap bars, body wash, shampoo, conditioner, and lotion manufacturers requiring skin-safe, rinse-stable formulas. In home fragrance, we supply candle manufacturers working in soy, paraffin, and coconut wax, plus reed diffuser producers requiring high-load, low-viscosity compounds. We also serve incense and bakhoor manufacturers, industrial cleaning product brands, and car care product producers. Our client base spans raw material manufacturers, contract packagers, private label brands, and FMCG companies across the GCC, Africa, South Asia, and beyond.

What documentation comes with bulk fragrance oil orders?

Every bulk fragrance oil order from Scent Bazaar ships with a complete documentation package. The Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS) provides full hazard classification, safe handling instructions, first aid procedures, and storage requirements for your health and safety records. The Certificate of Analysis (COA) confirms the batch-specific physical and chemical properties of the oil — appearance, colour, odour, specific gravity, and refractive index — against the product specification. An allergen declaration identifies any EU-listed fragrance allergens present above the declarable threshold, which is essential for cosmetic and personal care product labelling compliance. For air freight and ocean freight consignments where the fragrance oil is classified as a flammable liquid under IATA or IMDG regulations, we provide the corresponding Dangerous Goods documentation. Additional certificates such as Halal, cruelty-free, or country-specific import declarations are available on request.

Your branded containers carry your logo, product name, batch reference, and all required safety information — indistinguishable from products made in your own facility. How is bulk fragrance oil pricing calculated?

Bulk pricing is calculated across clearly defined volume tiers — the more you order, the lower your per-kilogram cost. The base tier begins at our 100ml minimum order quantity for standard wholesale oils, with pricing reducing meaningfully at 1kg, 5kg, 25kg drum, and full-pallet levels. Beyond volume, pricing is influenced by fragrance complexity and raw material cost: oud-based, natural-heavy, and botanical-rich formulas carry higher per-kilogram rates than synthetic-dominant commercial accords, simply because their raw ingredient costs are substantially greater. Packaging format also affects the total price — steel drums cost more than HDPE jerry cans, for instance. For contracted repeat buyers, we offer locked pricing arrangements against volume commitments, which gives your production planning cost certainty. Contact us on WhatsApp with your target fragrance type, volume, application, and packaging preference, and we will provide a detailed tiered pricing sheet within one business day.

Can you supply fragrance oils that are stable in high-pH cleaning products?

Yes. Our industrial fragrance range includes pH-stable compounds specifically engineered to survive alkaline manufacturing environments, where a standard perfume oil would discolour, phase-separate, or lose scent character within days of production. Cleaning product formulations — including liquid detergents, powder detergents, fabric softeners, surface cleaners, toilet blocks, and dishwashing liquids — typically operate at pH levels between 8 and 12, which destroys most fine fragrance compounds. Our chemists, led by founder Nawar Mohammed who holds a background in chemical engineering, select and where necessary reformulate compounds using alkali-stable aromatic materials and appropriate fixatives. We can supply oils with pH stability certificates confirming performance at your target alkaline range. If you are developing a new cleaning product or reformulating an existing one, share your formulation matrix with us and we will recommend the most technically compatible oils from our catalogue.

What is the lead time for a large bulk fragrance oil order from Dubai?

For fragrance oils held in stock at our Dubai warehouse, bulk orders can be prepared, drummed into your chosen packaging format, documented, and dispatched within 3 to 7 business days, depending on the total volume and whether custom labelling is required. For custom-formulated bulk orders, lead time begins from formula sign-off and is typically 2 to 4 weeks to production-ready dispatch, accounting for raw material procurement, blending, quality testing, and drumming. Once dispatched from Dubai, ocean freight transit times vary by destination: GCC ports receive shipments within 2 to 5 days, East Africa and South Asia typically 7 to 14 days, and Europe 15 to 25 days depending on routing. Air freight options are available for urgent orders where the volume and fragrance classification permit.
