Frequently Asked Questions

Do you supply raw aroma chemicals as well as pre-blended oils?

Yes. Alongside our library of 2,000+ finished fragrance compounds, we are a major supplier of raw aroma chemicals, essential oils, absolutes, and resins used by master perfumers to build scents from scratch.

Are your fragrance oils IFRA compliant?

Absolutely. All our high-quality fragrance oils are manufactured in accordance with strict International Fragrance Association (IFRA) guidelines to ensure they are safe for skin contact and commercial distribution.

Can you ship your fragrance oils outside of the UAE?

Yes, Scent Bazaar is a major global exporter. We ship bulk and wholesale orders to over 50 countries worldwide, providing all the necessary hazardous materials documentation and MSDS sheets for customs clearance.

Where is your physical supply office located?

We are physically located in the heart of Dubai's traditional perfume hub. You can visit our perfume store in Deira at Souq Naif, Shop 47, to smell our oils in person and discuss your brand's needs.

What types of fragrance oils do you supply for cosmetic applications?

We supply body-safe, skin-contact fragrance oils formulated for cosmetic use including body lotions, shower gels, hair care, and roll-on perfumes. All cosmetic-grade oils meet IFRA skin-contact limits and are supplied with full safety documentation including MSDS and allergen declarations required by UAE and EU cosmetic regulations.

What is the difference between a fragrance oil and an essential oil?

Essential oils are 100% natural extracts distilled or cold-pressed directly from plants, flowers, or resins. Fragrance oils are engineered aroma compounds — they may be fully synthetic, partially natural, or a blend — offering greater scent complexity, colour stability, and cost efficiency. Most commercial perfumes use high-quality fragrance oils rather than pure essential oils due to superior performance and consistency.
