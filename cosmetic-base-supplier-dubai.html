Frequently Asked Questions

How much fragrance oil can I add to your lotion base?

Our premium unscented lotion bases are formulated to remain stable while carrying a high fragrance load. Depending on the specific oil and its molecular composition, you can generally blend between 2% and 5% fragrance oil by weight without causing emulsion breakdown or phase separation. Heavier oriental and oud-based oils tend to perform best at the lower end of this range — around 2% to 3% — while lighter floral and citrus compounds can comfortably reach 4% to 5%. If you are working with Scent Bazaar's own perfume oil catalogue, our technical team can advise on the optimal load rate for each specific oil to ensure your finished product remains stable, aesthetically pleasing, and skin safe throughout its intended shelf life.

Are your cosmetic bases skin safe and suitable for commercial use?

Yes. All our cosmetic bases are manufactured to strict industry standards and are fully suitable for commercial product development. Each base is formulated without parabens, sulphates, or artificial colourants, and is tested to confirm it is hypoallergenic and safe for topical skin contact across all skin types. Every batch comes with a Certificate of Analysis (COA) and Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS) confirming ingredient purity and compliance. Our bases meet the requirements of UAE cosmetics regulations and are compatible with EU Cosmetic Regulation 1223/2009 for brands planning to export to European markets. Whether you are producing body lotions, hand creams, or shower gels for retail, hospitality supply, or private label export, our bases provide a professionally validated starting point for your formulation.

Do you supply packaging for lotions and shower gels?

Yes. Scent Bazaar is an end-to-end supplier and we stock a range of packaging formats specifically designed for cosmetic bases. For lotions and shower gels, we carry high-quality PET plastic and glass bottles in sizes from 100ml to 500ml, fitted with professional pump dispensers, disc-top caps, and flip-top closures suitable for high-viscosity liquids. For thicker cream and balm formulations, we supply wide-mouth glass and PET cream jars with inner lids. All containers are compatible with our base formulations and require no additional lining or treatment. Sourcing your bases and packaging from us in a single order simplifies procurement, reduces lead time, and ensures component compatibility — particularly important for leak-free export shipments to buyers across the GCC and beyond.

Growing brands can order in 10-litre and 25-litre containers with tiered wholesale pricing. For established manufacturers and contract production facilities in the UAE and GCC, we supply on a bulk basis with volume pricing available on request. All quantities ship with full MSDS and COA documentation. Contact our team via WhatsApp to receive a current wholesale price list and discuss the right order format for your production scale and product range.

Are your cosmetic bases paraben-free and cruelty-free?

Yes. Our complete cosmetic base range is manufactured to be paraben-free, sulphate-free, and produced entirely without animal testing, making them suitable for cruelty-free certified product lines. All formulations use modern, consumer-safe preservation systems that meet current clean beauty standards without compromising microbiological stability or shelf life. This is increasingly important for brands targeting GCC retail chains, European export markets, and e-commerce platforms where paraben-free and vegan-friendly claims are prominently expected. Each base comes with COA documentation that confirms the absence of restricted substances. Our technical team, led by a chemical engineer with deep formulation expertise, can advise on any additional certification requirements for your specific target market or retail channel.

Can your cosmetic bases be used with natural or organic fragrance oils?

Yes. Our cosmetic bases are fully compatible with natural essential oils, organic fragrance blends, and synthetic perfume compounds. However, natural and organic oils do require slightly different dilution ratios compared to synthetic compounds. As a general guideline, natural essential oils perform best at 1% to 3% in our lotion and wash bases, whereas synthetic fragrance oils — which are more concentrated and stable — can be loaded at up to 5% without affecting emulsion integrity. Some natural botanical extracts can also affect the colour or texture of the finished base, so we recommend small test batches before scaling to full production. Our team is available via WhatsApp to provide specific compatibility guidance for any oil from our catalogue of over 2,000 fragrance compounds.

Do your cosmetic bases come with safety documentation for regulatory compliance?

Yes. Every cosmetic base order from Scent Bazaar includes a Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS) and a Certificate of Analysis (COA) as standard. The MSDS covers full ingredient disclosure, hazard classifications, safe storage and handling requirements, and first-aid guidance. The COA confirms batch-specific quality metrics including pH, viscosity, and microbial test results. These documents are essential for product registration with UAE regulatory authorities and for compliance with EU Cosmetic Regulation 1223/2009 if you are exporting to European markets. They also support product liability insurance applications and retail buyer due diligence requirements. Documentation is supplied digitally and can be made available in formats acceptable to customs and import authorities in over 50 countries we currently ship to.

Can I private label cosmetic base products under my own brand?

Yes. Scent Bazaar offers a full private label cosmetic manufacturing service where we formulate, scent, fill, and label finished products under your own brand name. Product categories include body lotions, hand creams, shower gels, shampoos, and solid perfume balms. The process begins with selecting your base formulation and choosing a fragrance from our catalogue of over 2,000 perfume oils. We then handle custom fragrance integration, filling into your chosen packaging, and label application. We can also assist with label design coordination and export-ready packaging to meet the requirements of retail buyers and import authorities across the GCC, Africa, and Europe. It is a turnkey solution suited to beauty brands, hotel amenities suppliers, and retail chains looking to launch or expand their own-brand fragrance product lines.

Are your cosmetic bases suitable for sensitive and allergy-prone skin?

Our cosmetic bases are formulated to be hypoallergenic and free from the most common skin irritants — no parabens, sulphates, synthetic dyes, or formaldehyde-releasing preservatives. They are well suited for sensitive skin product lines, including baby skincare, post-procedure recovery creams, and fragrance-free body lotions. We always recommend performing a patch test protocol before any new finished product launch, particularly when fragrance compounds have been added. For allergy-prone consumers, we can supply bases with a reduced allergen fragrance profile, selecting perfume compounds that minimise known sensitising molecules. Our technical team, with a chemical engineering background, can advise on ingredient substitutions and formulation adjustments to help you meet dermatologist-tested or hypoallergenic label claims if required for your target retail channel.

Are your cosmetic bases stable in the heat of the Middle East and Africa?

Yes. Our cosmetic bases are specifically tested for thermal stability and are designed to perform reliably in the hot climates of the GCC, North Africa, and Sub-Saharan Africa — markets we actively supply. The emulsification systems used in our lotion and cream bases are selected to maintain consistency and prevent phase separation at ambient temperatures up to 40°C, which is typical of indoor storage conditions in Dubai and across the region during summer. We recommend storing bases in a cool, shaded environment away from direct sunlight and heat sources to maximise shelf life, but our formulations are robust enough to withstand routine warm-climate warehousing. Exporters shipping to tropical or equatorial markets can order with confidence that the base will arrive and store in stable condition.

What types of cosmetic bases do you supply?

We supply four core cosmetic base categories. Our unscented body lotion base is a fast-absorbing, lightweight emulsion suited to daily moisturising products. Our melt-and-pour soap bases come in both transparent and opaque options, ready for fragrance and colour addition without lye handling. Our liquid wash bases cover shower gels, hand wash, and shampoo formulations, all pre-thickened and preservative-stable. Finally, our solid perfume and balm bases use wax and shea butter blends for crafting solid fragrance sticks, lip balms, and multi-purpose body butters. All four categories are available in 5-litre, 10-litre, and 25-litre containers, with bulk supply on request. Each base is supplied with MSDS and COA documentation and is fully compatible with our range of over 2,000 fragrance oils.
