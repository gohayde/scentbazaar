# ScentBazaar Content Calendar: April 2026 – March 2027

**Site:** https://scentbazaar.co/
**Business:** B2B Wholesale Fragrance Supplier — Deira, Dubai
**Created:** March 2026
**Current SEO Score:** 58/100 | Content Quality: 52/100
**Goal:** Reach 75/100 overall score by March 2027 through systematic content creation

---

## Strategic Overview

ScentBazaar has a technically sound 15-page service site but zero editorial content. The site claims 2,000+ fragrance oils yet lists none of them. It has 172 Google reviews embedded in schema but invisible to visitors. There is no About page, no blog, no Arabic content, and no content targeting the educational queries that B2B buyers search before they contact a supplier.

This calendar addresses those gaps in priority order: fix trust signals first, launch the blog second, scale internationally third, and build authority fourth.

**Publishing commitment required: 3–4 pieces per month minimum.** This is achievable for a small team using a mix of in-house knowledge (the store's staff know this industry deeply) and a single freelance SEO writer on retainer.

### April 2026 — Month 1

#### F-01: About ScentBazaar
- **Target URL:** `/about`
- **Primary keyword:** fragrance supplier Dubai (1,200 searches/mo est.)
- **Secondary keywords:** wholesale fragrance company UAE, perfume supplier Deira, B2B fragrance distributor Dubai
- **Target persona:** New-to-market private label entrepreneur researching suppliers; international export buyer doing due diligence
- **Word count:** 1,200–1,500 words
- **Content type:** New page
- **Must include:**
  - Company founding story and years in operation
  - Physical address and showroom details (Deira location is a trust signal)
  - Team/founder credentials or background
  - The 2,000+ fragrance library claim with context (how it is sourced, how often updated)
  - Supplier relationships (French house fragrances, Arabic heritage materials)
  - Photo of the store/team (critical for E-E-A-T)
  - Call to action: WhatsApp + visit us
- **Priority rationale:** About pages are the single highest E-E-A-T ROI available. Google's quality rater guidelines explicitly treat missing About pages as a trust deficiency. International B2B buyers will not place a first order without this page. Every other content effort is diminished without it.

#### F-02: Customer Reviews & Testimonials
- **Target URL:** `/reviews`
- **Primary keyword:** ScentBazaar reviews (branded + trust)
- **Secondary keywords:** fragrance supplier reviews Dubai, wholesale perfume supplier testimonials UAE
- **Target persona:** Any prospect doing final due diligence before first contact
- **Word count:** 800–1,000 words (framing copy) + embedded reviews
- **Content type:** New page
- **Must include:**
  - The 172 Google reviews surfaced on-page (embed widget or static pull)
  - Segmented testimonials by customer type: brand founders, candle makers, hoteliers, export buyers
  - At least 3 case study excerpts (even brief: "Client X ordered X, launched in 3 months")
  - Star rating schema (already partially in place — display it)
  - Google Business Profile link
- **Priority rationale:** 172 reviews exist in schema but are invisible to site visitors and to AI search engines that look for on-page social proof. This is a zero-cost trust fix waiting to happen.

### May 2026 — Month 2

#### F-03: Shipping, Export & Compliance Guide
- **Target URL:** `/shipping-compliance`
- **Primary keyword:** fragrance export from Dubai (long-tail, high buyer intent)
- **Secondary keywords:** dangerous goods packing fragrance UAE, perfume oil export regulations, IFRA compliance Dubai, alcohol-based perfume shipping UAE
- **Target persona:** Export buyer (India, Africa, Europe) placing first international order; candle maker needing SDS documentation
- **Word count:** 2,000–2,500 words
- **Content type:** New page (long-form guide)
- **Must include:**
  - Minimum order quantities by product type
  - Packaging standards for DG (dangerous goods) air and sea freight
  - IFRA compliance: what it is, which products carry IFRA certificates
  - SDS/MSDS availability and how to request
  - Countries served and typical lead times
  - Payment terms and customs documentation ScentBazaar provides
  - FAQ section (feeds FAQ schema)
- **Priority rationale:** The single most common reason B2B international buyers abandon a supplier is inability to find shipping and compliance information. This page directly converts export inquiry intent.

#### F-04: Fragrance Oil Catalog
- **Target URL:** `/fragrance-catalog`
- **Primary keyword:** fragrance oil catalog Dubai (medium volume, very high intent)
- **Secondary keywords:** buy fragrance oils UAE, fragrance oil list wholesale, oud fragrance oil wholesale Dubai, floral fragrance oil supplier UAE
- **Target persona:** Perfume brand founder building a line; candle maker sourcing new scents; cosmetics manufacturer seeking fragrance input
- **Word count:** 1,500 words (intro/guide copy) + catalog structure
- **Content type:** New page
- **Structure:**
  - Scent family index: Woody, Oriental/Oud, Floral, Fresh/Aquatic, Gourmand, Citrus, Fougère, Chypre, Aromatic, Musk/Amber
  - Spotlight 10–15 bestsellers per family with brief description
  - Filter/browse UX by family, note, origin (French, Arabic, etc.)
  - Link to WhatsApp/contact for full list or sampling
  - Note on minimum order for sampling vs. bulk
- **Priority rationale:** Claiming 2,000+ oils while showing zero of them is the biggest credibility gap on the site. This catalog does not need to be fully searchable on day one — even a curated showcase of 50–100 scents by family fixes the gap and creates enormous long-tail keyword surface area.

#### F-05: FAQ Page
- **Target URL:** `/faq`
- **Primary keyword:** wholesale fragrance questions UAE
- **Secondary keywords:** minimum order fragrance oil Dubai, how to order perfume wholesale UAE, ScentBazaar MOQ
- **Target persona:** First-time B2B buyer at the research stage
- **Word count:** 1,000–1,200 words
- **Content type:** New page (FAQ schema)
- **Questions to cover:**
  - What is the minimum order quantity?
  - Do you supply internationally?
  - Can I get samples before ordering?
  - Do you provide IFRA certificates and SDS sheets?
  - Can you create a custom fragrance blend?
  - What is the difference between perfume base and perfumers alcohol?
  - How long does private label take?
  - Do you offer white-label packaging?
  - What payment methods do you accept?
  - Where are you located / can I visit?
- **Priority rationale:** FAQ schema already partially used on service pages. A central FAQ page consolidates these signals, ranks for question-form queries, and is explicitly recommended by Google for AI Overviews eligibility.

## Content Format Mix

### Long-Form Guides (1,500–3,000 words)
**Best for:** High-intent B2B keywords, pillar content, backlink acquisition, AI Overview eligibility

Priority examples: B-01 (Private Label Brand), B-03 (IFRA Compliance), B-12 (Export Shipping), TL-01 (Industry Report), F-03 (Shipping Guide)

Each long-form guide should include:
- A table of contents with anchor links
- At least one downloadable asset (PDF checklist, spec sheet template, or comparison table)
- 3–5 internal links to service pages
- FAQ section with schema markup
- Author attribution with credentials

**Monthly allocation: 1–2 long-form guides per month**

---

### Short Informational Posts (800–1,200 words)
**Best for:** Seasonal hooks, question-based queries, social repurposing, lower production cost

Priority examples: B-08 (MOQ guide), B-22 (UAE National Day), B-27 (Ramadan logistics)

**Monthly allocation: 1–2 short posts per month during phases 2–4**

---

## Resources and Workflow

### Recommended Team
- **Internal:** ScentBazaar staff member owns content calendar, provides industry knowledge and product expertise, approves all content
- **External:** 1 SEO copywriter (Arabic-English bilingual preferred) on monthly retainer for blog posts
- **External:** 1 photographer/videographer for quarterly shoot (store, products, blending process)
- **Tools:** Google Search Console (already should be set up), Ahrefs or Semrush for keyword tracking, a simple CMS or static site generator for the blog

### Static Site Consideration
The current site is static HTML. Adding a blog at scale requires either:
1. A simple static site generator (Hugo, Eleventy, Jekyll) — preserves current architecture, generates blog pages from Markdown files
2. A headless CMS (Contentful, Sanity) with static export — allows non-technical content editing
3. A subdomain blog (blog.scentbazaar.co on WordPress) — fastest to launch but splits domain authority

**Recommendation:** Hugo or Eleventy for the blog, matching the existing static HTML approach. The blog content lives in `/blog/` on the main domain, preserving all link equity.

### Content Production Workflow
1. Keyword brief created (2-week lead time before publish date)
2. Outline approved by ScentBazaar
3. Draft written by copywriter
4. Technical/product accuracy review by ScentBazaar staff
5. SEO review: internal links, title tag, meta description, schema, images
6. Publish + submit URL to Google Search Console for indexing
7. Share on Instagram, TikTok, WhatsApp Business
