## Top 10 Prioritized Actions

### Critical
1. **Verify and sync business hours across all platforms** — Website shows 8:30am open time; some search results returned 10am. If GBP shows wrong hours, Google may demote the listing and customers will get false info. Check GBP → Info → Hours and match exactly to website.

2. **Claim Bing Places listing** — Bing Webmaster data is unverifiable without a claimed profile. Go to [bingplaces.com](https://bingplaces.com) and import your Google Business Profile. Bing feeds Cortana, Bing Maps, and LinkedIn local data.

3. **Claim Apple Business Connect** — Apple Maps is the default map on all iPhones. International B2B buyers (US, UK, Europe) use Apple Maps. Claim at [businessconnect.apple.com](https://businessconnect.apple.com). NAP must match GBP exactly.

### High
4. **Address brand confusion from "The Scent Bazaar" (Delhi) and scentbazaar.shop** — Add your GBP description and Q&A specifically to clarify "Dubai-based wholesale supplier." Consider filing a report on GBP if the Delhi listing appears in Maps searches for your brand. Monitor using Google Alerts for "Scent Bazaar reviews."

5. **Implement the LocalBusiness JSON-LD schema above** — The website is missing `geo` coordinates and `sameAs` social links in structured data. This directly feeds Google's Knowledge Panel and Maps listing enrichment.

6. **Activate GBP owner responses** — Reply to every review (especially the 162 existing ones' most recent batch). Google's algorithm favors profiles with active owner engagement. Target: respond to all reviews within 24 hours. Template: acknowledge the product used, thank them by name, invite them back.

### Medium
7. **Upload 20+ GBP photos across 4 categories** — Interior (showroom ambiance), Products (oil bottles, sample kits, private label examples), Exterior (shop front with signage), Team (Nawar + staff). Profiles with 10+ photos receive 42% more direction requests (Google data).

8. **Populate GBP Q&A section proactively** — Pre-seed with the 5–8 questions B2B buyers always ask: "What is your MOQ?", "Do you ship internationally?", "Can I get samples before ordering?", "Do you offer private label?", "What turnaround time for private label?" Answer them yourself via GBP owner account.

9. **Add secondary GBP categories** — Primary is likely "Perfume Store." Add: "Wholesale Grocer" (closest UAE match), "Essential Oil Supplier," "Cosmetics Wholesaler." Multiple relevant categories expand keyword triggers on Maps.

10. **Add Scent Bazaar to OpenStreetMap** — Many B2B routing apps, travel apps, and mapping libraries pull from OSM. Visit [openstreetmap.org](https://openstreetmap.org) and add the business with tag `shop=perfumery` + `wholesale=yes` + `name=Scent Bazaar`.
