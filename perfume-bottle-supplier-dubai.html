Integrated Supply

Why Source Packaging from Your Oil Supplier?

The primary advantage of using a combined oil manufacturer and packaging trader is component compatibility. If you buy bottles from Vendor A and pumps from Vendor B, you run a huge risk of the pump failing to crimp securely over the glass neck, ruining your entire production run.

At ScentBazaar, every bottle is pre-tested against our specific atomizers. Furthermore, if you take advantage of our perfume filling service or full private label manufacturing, our technicians calibrate the automated filling lines precisely to the bottles and caps you selected from our inventory.

This guarantees an airtight, factory-grade seal with zero leakage before the perfumes are shrink-wrapped and dispatched from Dubai to the global market.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to buy huge quantities of bottles at once?

No. By sourcing from our Dubai warehouse, you avoid the massive 10,000+ unit MOQs demanded by overseas factories. We supply startups with runs as small as 500 units, allowing you to test the market efficiently.

Can you print my logo directly onto the glass bottle?

Yes. As part of our comprehensive private label service, we offer screen printing, hot foil stamping, and high-quality metallic decal application directly onto the glass hardware.

Do you sell the outer cardboard packaging as well?

Yes, we supply complete turnkey packaging, including the inner rigid box, the outer carton sleeve, and the final protective cellophane wrap used in luxury retail.

Do your spray pumps require a crimping machine?

We primarily stock crimp-on atomizers because they provide the safest, most leak-proof seal for fine fragrances. If you are manufacturing manually, we offer affordable hand-crimping tools, or you can outsource the process entirely via our automated filling machines.
