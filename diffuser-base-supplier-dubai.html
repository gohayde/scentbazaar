Optimal Scent Throw

High-Performance Diffuser Solvents

Creating a successful luxury home fragrance product requires mastering the "throw" (how far the scent travels) without causing the product to evaporate within a week. As a specialized diffuser base supplier in Dubai, Scent Bazaar provides the technical solvents necessary to achieve this exact balance.

A reed diffuser works via capillary action, pulling the liquid up the porous rods. If you mix your fragrance oil with standard alcohol or water, it will evaporate too fast or fail to travel up the reeds entirely. Our premium diffuser bases are formulated with eco-friendly solvents (such as Augeo Clean Multi) that dramatically slow down evaporation while thinning the fragrance oil enough to wick perfectly.

These advanced bases are non-toxic, have an extremely low carbon footprint, and produce virtually zero residual odor, allowing the true profile of your chosen fragrance to shine through.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best ratio for mixing oils with a diffuser base?

For optimal scent throw without clogging the reeds, the industry standard is to blend 20% to 25% high-quality fragrance oil with 75% to 80% of our premium reed diffuser base.

Do I need to add water or alcohol to your base?

No. Our diffuser bases are pre-formulated and ready to use. Adding water will cause the oil to separate and turn cloudy, and adding alcohol will cause the mixture to evaporate far too quickly.

Are your diffuser bases safe to use indoors?

Yes. We prioritize modern, eco-friendly solvents (like Augeo) that have a very low vapor pressure, are non-toxic to breath, and are fully compliant with global VOC (Volatile Organic Compound) regulations.

Do you supply the actual diffuser reeds as well?

Yes, we are a complete home fragrance hardware supplier. We stock natural rattan reeds (which offer a rustic look) and high-performance engineered Dacron/fiber reeds (which offer superior capillary flow and do not require flipping).

How long will a 100ml reed diffuser last when using your base?

A 100ml reed diffuser with our premium base and standard rattan reeds typically provides consistent fragrance dispersion for 4 to 6 weeks under normal indoor conditions (with a 20-25% oil load). If you flip the reeds weekly and use engineered fiber reeds, diffusion duration can extend toward 8 weeks of soft ambient scenting.

Can your diffuser base be used in electric ultrasonic diffusers?

No. Our reed diffuser bases are engineered for passive capillary diffusion only and should not be used in ultrasonic or nebulizing diffusers, as the solvents and fixatives are incompatible with heat and ultrasonic vibration, which will damage the device and degrade the fragrance.
