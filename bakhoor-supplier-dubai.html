The Art of Fumigation

Authentic Bakhoor & Dukhoon Manufacturing

In Middle Eastern culture, scenting the home and clothing with smoldering incense is a sign of ultimate hospitality and prestige. As a deeply rooted Bakhoor supplier in Dubai, Scent Bazaar masterfully blends traditional techniques with modern, consistent manufacturing protocols to deliver premium incense to global retailers.

We supply two primary categories of traditional incense. Dukhoon is created by grinding premium Oud wood and sandalwood into a fine powder, mixing it with binding resins, sugar, and heavy arabic fragrance oils, and pressing it into tablets. Oud Ma'attar involves taking pure, raw agarwood chips and soaking them deep in concentrated attars for months to absorb the scent.

Our Deira factory produces both styles in massive wholesale quantities, ensuring perfectly balanced moisture content so the incense smolders slowly on charcoal rather than burning up instantly.

Raw Materials

Pure Oud Chips and Resins

For brands and artisans looking to manufacture their own incense, Scent Bazaar functions as a vital supplier of the raw, foundational materials required for authentic Arabian perfumery.

  • Agarwood (Oud) Chips: We import graded Indian, Cambodian, and Vietnamese agarwood. We supply both pure chips for direct burning and lower-grade structural chips meant to be crushed into powder for Dukhoon bases.
  • Raw Resins & Musks: Authentic Bakhoor requires heavy fixatives to bind the powder and create the characteristic dense smoke. We supply bulk Frankincense (Luban), Myrrh, and raw deer musk accords.
  • Concentrated Attars: The liquid profile of the incense. We offer highly concentrated versions of Rose Taifi, Saffron, Amber, and Mukhallat bulk oils formulated specifically for high-heat applications.

Incense Knowledge

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Bakhoor, Dukhoon, and pure Oud?

Pure Oud refers to unadulterated agarwood chips burned for their natural scent. 'Bakhoor' is a general term for scented/soaked woodchips (often called Oud Ma'attar). 'Dukhoon' specifically refers to wood powder that is mixed with oils and pressed into a solid tablet or ball.

Do you sell the burners (Mabkhara) and charcoal?

Yes. As a comprehensive supplier, we stock wholesale quantities of traditional wooden, ceramic, and modern electronic incense burners (Mabkhara) alongside quick-lighting charcoal specifically designed for incense.

Can you create a custom Bakhoor scent for my brand?

Absolutely. You can select any fragrance oil from our catalog—even modern French profiles—and our lab will formulate it into a custom Dukhoon tablet or soaked woodchip for your private label brand.

Are there customs restrictions on shipping Oud wood internationally?

Real agarwood is subject to CITES (Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species) regulations. As experienced exporters, Scent Bazaar handles the necessary certifications or formulates high-fidelity synthetic alternatives to ensure smooth customs clearance.

How long should quality Bakhoor burn on charcoal?

Quality Bakhoor should burn slowly and consistently for 20 to 40 minutes per piece on properly heated charcoal. Burn time depends on the density of the woodchip and the oil load. Our premium Oud Ma'attar products are formulated to give long, rich, non-acrid burns without excessive smoke, releasing fragrance that lingers in a space for hours.

Is your Bakhoor suitable for gifting and luxury hospitality?

Absolutely. Our premium Bakhoor range is presented in elegant, gift-ready packaging, making it ideal for luxury hospitality gifting, corporate gifts, and high-end retail. Many five-star hotels, royal households, and premium lifestyle brands in the GCC and internationally source their exclusive Bakhoor from Scent Bazaar for ambient scenting and guest gifting.
